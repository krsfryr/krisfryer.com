Impartner Marketplace

Designing Impartner's Marketplace was about transforming business connections into seamless, engaging experiences. By focusing on user-centric design, I created an intuitive platform that fosters meaningful partnerships and redefines how businesses connect.

Role: UX & UI Designer, Front End Developer

Company: Impartner

Crafting Connections Through UX Innovation

In the world of digital marketplaces, connecting businesses with the right partners is more than a transaction—it's an experience. At Impartner, I took on the challenge of designing Impartner Marketplace, creating a seamless, intuitive platform that fosters meaningful partnerships. This project was about more than just improving usability; it was about imagining a new way for businesses to connect through dynamic, user-centric design.

Collaborating with a cross-functional team, including the Product Manager, back-end, and front-end developers, we ensured the design met both business goals and technical requirements. By prioritizing usability, design consistency, and mobile accessibility, we created a platform that significantly improved engagement and streamlined user workflows.

Software

Defining the Challenge: From Functionality to Flow

No Centralized Platform for Users to Create Partner Marketplaces

Impartner customers were constrained to purchasing the Partner Locator custom module, which required the internal development team to manually build each partner marketplace. This process was slow and inflexible, often necessitating further involvement from Impartner for updates. Additionally, this approach led to the creation of hundreds of unique codebases—one for each client—posing significant maintenance challenges for Impartner.

Creating a Universal Embeddable Design

Developing a universally embeddable design required extensive research to determine essential features and optional enhancements. The goal was to create a robust UI that could seamlessly integrate into a wide range of websites. A key focus was on simplicity while ensuring the design was flexible enough to accommodate the unique needs of different customers. Balancing ease of use with customization options was crucial in achieving a versatile and adaptable marketplace solution.

Problem Statement

How might we create an engaging and easy-to-use applocation that gives users the ability to create and maintain their partner ecosystem?

Main Features and Structure

Wireframes

Wireframes were instrumental in establishing the foundation for the user interface and experience of the partner marketplace. These low-fidelity designs allowed us to concentrate on structure, layout, and functionality without the distractions of visual design elements. Acting as a blueprint, the wireframes facilitated collaboration with stakeholders and ensured alignment on the core features and application flow. This stage was crucial in validating our approach before progressing to higher-fidelity designs and development.

Search Results

The focus here is on empowering admins with the tools to tailor the partner marketplace to specific needs. In the top portion, within the "Marketplace Studio" tab, admins can select which filters will be available for visitors to narrow their search results. This level of customization allows for a more precise search experience, enabling users to find partners that best match their criteria with ease. The wireframe also showcases a multi-faceted filter system, which is presented alongside a clean, organized grid layout. This design not only enhances usability by making it easy for users to view multiple results at once but also ensures a streamlined and efficient search process.

Partner Listing

This wireframe emphasizes the customization options available to admins in the "Marketplace Studio" tab, where they can choose which sections to display for each partner listing. By carefully selecting the content sections, admins can personalize the information that partners present, ensuring that the most relevant details are highlighted. The layout of the partner listing page begins with the partner's name and contact information at the top, followed by sections such as an overview, key features, and client success stories. This thoughtful structure is designed not only to inform visitors but also to engage them, encouraging a deeper exploration of each partner's unique offerings.

Hi-Fi Design

With the wireframes validated and feedback incorporated, we moved into the high-fidelity (hi-fi) design phase. Leveraging our established design system, we seamlessly applied consistent typography and color schemes, ensuring brand alignment and visual harmony across the application. Since this particular application doesn't require its own imagery, the design remains clean and focused, with any images being client-uploaded to their respective partner listings.

This hi-fi design represents the final iteration of the partner marketplace, where key functionality is paired with a polished, user-friendly interface. The design emphasizes clarity and ease of use, maintaining a consistent experience from search results to partner listings. By adhering to the design system and focusing on functionality, the final product delivers an efficient, cohesive user experience without unnecessary distractions.

Design Iterations

According to the feedback from our early-access clients and meetings, we gained helpful feedback and then iterated design based on what we learned and client requests. We continue to iterate on the functionality and design to ensure it is easy to use and has all the features our clients expect and need.

Final Design

The final design phase brought together all the insights gained from the previous iterations, resulting in a cohesive and user-friendly partner marketplace. The design system ensured consistency throughout, applying established typography and color guidelines to create a clean and professional interface. Each element—from search filters to partner listings—was fine-tuned for both functionality and aesthetics. This final product effectively balances visual clarity with the application's primary focus: usability. The design allows users to easily navigate the marketplace while providing admins the flexibility to customize partner content and filters. This combination delivers a refined user experience that meets both user and business needs.

1. Studio

The Studio section empowers admins with the tools they need to customize the marketplace. From this hub, admins can select the desired filters for visitors to use when narrowing their search results. These filters are fully customizable, allowing admins to tailor the user experience to the specific needs of the marketplace. The Studio interface is intuitive and straightforward, ensuring that admins can quickly adjust their settings and preview the changes in real time. This design prioritizes ease of use while maintaining the flexibility needed to meet diverse requirements.

2. Listing

The Listing section allows admins to control the content displayed for each partner. Through a series of customizable content blocks, admins can choose which sections to show, such as overviews, key features, client success stories, and more. The design is focused on clarity and organization, ensuring that visitors can easily access detailed partner information in a structured format. The final design is optimized for readability and user engagement, encouraging deeper exploration of each partner's offerings. The customization options in this section give admins the flexibility to tailor the presentation of their partners' content in a way that suits their marketplace's goals.

3. Embedded Marketplace

This example showcases the seamless integration of the partner marketplace into external websites, such as Impartner. It demonstrates how partners can display their content across different environments, increasing both visibility and accessibility. The final design ensures that embedded content retains the same consistent layout and functionality as in the main marketplace, while also adapting to the parent website by inheriting text styles.

In this example, the partner marketplace is embedded within the Impartner website, highlighting how easily the marketplace can extend its reach while maintaining a smooth user experience. This integration provides partners with a powerful way to showcase their offerings to a broader audience directly through their corporate website.

Key takeaways

The importance of flexibility in design for admin control

Throughout this project, I gained a deeper understanding of how important it is to provide admin users with flexible tools to customize content. Designing the Marketplace Studio required a balance between user experience and functionality, allowing admins to tailor the partner listings and search results to meet their unique needs. This reinforced the value of building interfaces that are adaptable without compromising on usability.

Streamlining the user experience without compromising functionality

One of the key takeaways from this project was learning how to streamline the user experience while maintaining essential features. In designing the search filters and partner listings, I ensured that the layout and navigation remained intuitive, even as more complex features were integrated. This challenged me to prioritize simplicity in user flow while providing robust functionality behind the scenes.
